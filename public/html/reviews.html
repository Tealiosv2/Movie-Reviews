Howl's Moving Castle

"A imaginative and breathtaking world."

► The animation and art style was breathtaking, really capturing the Studio Ghibli fantasy vibe. The film truly felt like a dream. The music was just as good, as expected from Studio Ghibli. However, the story, while starting off very strong, left me a little lost and detached in the end.

5/5 visuals 5/5 soundtrack 3/5 story

"Great film, a little confusing in the end..."

► The details in each frame are amazing, on second watch take the time to appreciate each scene. Each time the main theme returns on different instruments, brings a playful energy. The story is a kind journey about love, near the end it gets a little bit confusing without explanation.

5/5 visuals 5/5 soundtrack 4/5 story

Redline

"Cinematic masterpiece."

► Redline" is a cinematic masterpiece that takes you on an adrenaline-fueled ride through a visually stunning and audibly mesmerizing world. The animation and art style are nothing short of breathtaking, immersing the audience in a kaleidoscope of vibrant colors and meticulously crafted designs. From the moment the film begins, you'll find yourself captivated by its unique and dynamic aesthetic, reminiscent of the most visually striking anime works.

5/5 visuals 5/5 soundtrack 4/5 story

"An interesting watch."

► Redline was an interesting watch. The visuals were... well, they were there. The art style didn't really stand out, and it was definitely no Studio Ghibli. The movie felt like a disjointed daydream. As for the music, well, it existed too. But it didn't really leave any lasting impression. Now, the story had potential. It started with a bang but then kinda fizzled out. Overall, it was an experience, I guess.

4/5 visuals 4/5 soundtrack 4/5 story

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners

"Sad, sad ,sad. But good."

► Cyberpunk: Edgerunners" takes viewers on a visually stunning journey that rivals the enchanting artistry of Studio Ghibli. The animation and art style in this series are nothing short of breathtaking, immersing audiences in a futuristic world that's as captivating as it is detailed. Just like Studio Ghibli, "Cyberpunk: Edgerunners" manages to transport you into a dreamlike realm, where every frame is a work of art.

4/5 visuals 5/5 soundtrack 5/5 story

"Not a lasting impression."

► Cyberpunk Edgerunners had some interesting moments, but it didn't quite hit the mark for me. The visuals were alright, leaning towards the cyberpunk aesthetic, but they lacked the wow factor I was hoping for. The music was passable, but it didn't leave a lasting impression.

4/5 visuals 4/5 soundtrack 2/5 story
